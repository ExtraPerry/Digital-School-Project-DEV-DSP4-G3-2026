# SEO Keyword Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the search engine optimization (SEO) strategy for a peer-to-peer boat rental platform. The following analysis establishes the selection of the primary keyword and secondary terms designed to optimize the platform's organic search positioning.

Note : We cannot use another company name in any keyword.

## Keyword Placement

- **Primary keyword**: The primary keyword must be placed on the landing page (the page where users arrive).
- **Secondary keywords**: Secondary keywords can be placed on the "About Us" page.

## Primary Keyword

**`"Peer-to-peer boat rental"`**

### Strategic Rationale

This SEO strategy is built upon four strategic considerations that justify the selection of this term:

#### 1. Business Model Alignment

The platform's specifications define an **exclusively peer-to-peer business model**. The generic term "Boat rental" would result in direct competition with professional rental companies (Dream Yacht Charter) and boat clubs. The addition of **"peer-to-peer"** enables audience filtering to attract users specifically seeking this service model: competitive pricing, personable relationships, and enhanced flexibility.

#### 2. Targeted Search Intent

Queries containing this keyword demonstrate a **clear transactional intent**. Users are not merely seeking information or imagery but intend to complete a rental transaction. This query aligns with the expectations of two target segments:

- Competitive pricing (segment: **"Vacationers"**)
- Direct relationship with boat owners (segment: **"Enthusiasts"**)

#### 3. Differentiated Competitive Positioning

The generic term "Boat rental" exhibits an **extremely high level of competition**. Established players (Click&Boat, SamBoat) hold dominant positions that are challenging to displace. The "peer-to-peer" specification maintains significant search volume while targeting a qualified niche offering superior opportunities for rapid positioning.

#### 4. Semantic Architecture Foundation

This keyword constitutes the **semantic root** of the entire content strategy. It enables the development of geo-localized variants based on priority catchment areas:

- `Peer-to-peer boat rental Marseille`
- `Peer-to-peer boat rental Corsica`
- `Peer-to-peer boat rental La Rochelle`

## Secondary Keywords

The complementary lexical field reinforces the primary positioning through variants aligned with the platform's offering segments:

- **`"Private sailboat rental"`** (Segment: Enthusiasts)
- **`"Rent a cheap motorboat"`** (Segment: Vacationers)
- **`"Boat rental without skipper"`** (Licensed users)
