### Benchmark

#### 5. Which market(s) should be analyzed given the client's activity and request?

The main market to analyze is **nautical and collaborative boating** (sharing economy), specifically the **peer-to-peer boat rental segment (C2C)**.

This market is characterized by strong growth due to the trend of "usage rather than ownership" (a boat is expensive and rarely used). It is also marked by very strong seasonality (June to September in Europe) and specific legal constraints (insurance, safety equipment, permits).

#### 6. Who are the direct competitors you have identified?

_(You must provide the names of the identified competitors here)_

- **Click&Boat**: European leader, often called the "Airbnb of boats". Has a very large catalog and a high-performance mobile application.
- **SamBoat**: Major French player, strongly positioned on trust and secure insurance for owners.

#### 7. Who are the indirect competitors you have identified?

_(You must provide the names of the identified competitors here)_

- **Dream Yacht Charter**: Global professional charter company that owns its own fleet (unlike SailingLoc which has no inventory). The service is standardized but often more expensive.
- **Freedom Boat Club**: "Boat Club" model operating on a monthly subscription basis (no ownership or per-rental fee).

### Strategy Analysis

#### 8. Who is the main target identified through the market study?

_(Highlight here the socio-demographic information as well as the purchase and/or consumption motivations of the products or services offered)_

- **Profile**: "Nautical Vacationers". Men and women, 25-45 years old, middle to upper socio-professional categories.
- **Motivations**: Seeking an accessible leisure experience (lower price than professional rental) and simple for a day or a week. They often prefer motorboats for coastal outings or small sailboats. They are accustomed to digital platforms (like Airbnb).

#### 9. Who is the core target through the market study?

_(Highlight here the socio-demographic information as well as the purchase and/or consumption motivations of the products or services offered)_

- **Profile**: "Passionate Non-Owners". 30-55 years old, mostly men, holders of a boating license (coastal or offshore).
- **Motivations**: They know how to sail but do not wish to (or cannot) bear the costs of a boat year-round. They seek specific or technical boats (well-equipped owner's sailboats) and the authenticity of exchanging with another enthusiast (the owner). This is the most loyal and careful clientele.

#### 10. Who is the secondary target through the market study?

_(Highlight here the socio-demographic information as well as the purchase and/or consumption motivations of the products or services offered)_

- **Profile**: Boat Owners (Supply Side) and B2B Partners.
- **Motivations (Owners)**: Offset annual maintenance and mooring costs (approximately 10% of the boat's value per year). They need schedule management tools and reliable insurance.
- **Motivations (Partners)**: Insurance companies and chandlery stores wishing to access SailingLoc's qualified data to offer their products.
